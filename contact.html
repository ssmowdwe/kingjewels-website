QUICK ANSWERS

FREQUENTLY ASKED

HOW LONG IS SHIPPING?

Standard shipping takes 3–7 business days within the US. Express options are available at checkout. International shipping takes 7–14 days.

WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unworn items in original packaging. Custom and engraved pieces are final sale.

IS THE JEWELRY REAL GOLD?

Our pieces are gold plated (18K or 24K) over a high-quality base metal. We also offer gold vermeil options with a thicker plating layer for longer-lasting wear.

HOW DO I CARE FOR MY JEWELRY?

Store in a cool dry place, avoid water, perfume, and sweat. Polish gently with a soft cloth. Proper care extends the life of your plating significantly.

DO YOU OFFER CUSTOM PIECES?

Yes! We do custom pendants, engravings, iced-out pieces, and more. Visit our Custom Order page or reach out via this form to get started.

CAN I BUY NOW AND PAY LATER?

Yes, we offer Afterpay and Klarna at checkout. Split your purchase into 4 interest-free payments with no credit check required.
